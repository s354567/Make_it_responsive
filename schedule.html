Mental health

Date: 20th of November 2020

In this innovation camp you will learn about how to maintain a healthy mental health and why it is important to talk more about mental health.

Our guest speaker is the organisation "Mental helse".

They are a organization for all people with mental health problems, relatives and other interested parties. They work for a increased transparency, prevention of mental health problems and a better health service. They believe that it should be allowed to have a hard time and that mental health should be as easy to talk about as physical health. Mentalhelse.no
