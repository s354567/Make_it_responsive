Mental health

Date: 20th of November 2020

In this innovation camp you will learn about how to maintain a healthy mental health and why it is important to talk more about mental health.

Our guest speaker is the organisation "Mental helse".

They are a organization for all people with mental health problems, relatives and other interested parties. They work for a increased transparency, prevention of mental health problems and a better health service. They believe that it should be allowed to have a hard time and that mental health should be as easy to talk about as physical health. Mentalhelse.no

Technology of the future

Date: 28th of November 2020



In this innovation camp you will learn about the technology of the future, how robotics impacts mankind and what we as individuals can do to impact the global development of technology.

This course guest speaker will be mechanical engineer Christoph Keplinger.

Christoph Keplinger is an assistant professor and has a degree in physics from Johannes Kepler University Linz in Austria and has done research on mechanics and chemistry at Harvard. He was a TED Talk participant in 2018 and talked about how physics can challenge the current limitations of robotic hardware.
