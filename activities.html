Rodeo Drive

Photograph : Christian Hundley / TripSavvy

Rodeo Drive is a shopping street in Beverly Hills i Los Angeles i California. The street is known for its exclusive shops, with many of the world's finest and most expensive brands of clothing and jewelery represented. Underneath are of some of the stores you can find at Rodeo Drive:

Balenciaga, Chanel, Guess, Michael Kors, Prada, Rolex, Tom Ford, Saint Laurent, Versace, Zadig & Voltaire, Amiri, Bally, Burberry, David Webb, Dior, Gucci Berverly Hills.

Universal Studios Orlando

Photograph : unknown

Universal Studios Orlando is a theme park located at : 6000 Universal Blvd, Orlando, FL 32819, USA. This theme park was first opened June 7th, 1990. This is a park for everyone. It has many different types of rollercoaster, teather and much more. You can for example visit the Harry Potter part of the park were you can visit the Knight Bus or Ollivanders and buy yourself a wand.

Statue Of Liberty

Photograph : Reiselykke.no

The Statue Of Liberty is an American national monument on Liberty Island near New York.The Statue of Liberty is a gift from the French people to the United States. They began building it in September 1875 and not fully finished before 1885. The Statue located at New York, NY 10004, USA. The statue is 46 meters high, with a base 92 meters. The entrance to the shelf leads to 403 steps to the interior of the head, where there is room for 12 people. It also serves as a lighthouse.
